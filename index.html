About the Yorkshire Floral Company

Hello! The Yorkshire Floral Co was established in 2020. Having always had a flair for creativity we pride ourselves bringing out of the box ideas to life. We work with luxury seasonal fresh flowers as well as the highest quality silk faux flowers to create floral masterpieces tailored to suit your personal taste. Having flowered venues across Yorkshire and beyond we can recommend ways to enhance your event spaces.

My Services

Weddings

Weddings are our passion. Flowers are such an important factor of your wedding day and can be often the first thing your guests will see. We offer a free appointment to talk over your ideas and offer a no obligation quote. When booking we will create a mood board for your event and make your flower dreams come true.
