Our Story

Established in 2020, The Yorkshire Floral Co are passionate and dedicated to creating show stopping floral designs. Having always had a flair for creativity we pride ourselves on bringing out of the box ideas to life. We work with luxury seasonal fresh flowers as well as the highest quality silk faux flowers to create floral masterpieces tailored to suit your personal taste. Having flowered venues across the UK and beyond we can recommend ways to enhance your event spaces, while optimising your budget.

The Modern Day Florist, we are a young team and have the knowledge behind the current trends. Whether you are in need of a florist for your wedding day or require a bespoke creation for your home or your business, we pride ourselves on offering a personal customer experience. Welcome to The Yorkshire Floral Co, where every petal tells a story and every bloom is a brushstroke in the canvas of your unforgettable celebration. I'm Lucy, the visionary behind our enchanting floral designs.

At The Yorkshire Floral Co, we believe in more than just creating beautiful arrangements; we believe in crafting experiences that linger in the hearts and memories of our clients long after the last dance.

Our journey began with a dream to infuse weddings with the timeless elegance of nature while minimizing environmental impact. Each piece we create is a testament to this ethos, meticulously crafted from reusable materials and ethically sourced blooms. From enchanting bouquets to breathtaking installations, every design reflects our dedication to both artistry and sustainability.
