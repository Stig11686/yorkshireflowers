Our Story

Established in 2020, The Yorkshire Floral Co are passionate and dedicated to creating shoow stopping floral designs. Having always had a flair for creativity we pride ourselves on bringing out of the box ideas to life. We work with luxury seasonal fresh flowers as well as the highest quality silk faux flowers to create floral masterpieces tailored to suit your personal taste. Having flowered venues across the UK and beyond we can recommend ways to enhance your event spaces, while optimising your budget.

The Modern Day Florist, we are a young team and have the knowledge behind the current trends. Whether you are in need of a florist for your wedding day or require a bespoke creation for your home or your business, we pride ourselves on offering a personal customer experience.
