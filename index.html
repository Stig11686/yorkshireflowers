What I Do

Hello, my name is Lucy, a lass from Yorkshire and lover of all things floral. I set up The Yorkshire Floral Co in spring 2020; whilst on maternity leave with my second son. I have always loved being creative and really wanted a beautiful statement faux wreath for my front door but I was struggling to find one, so I decided to get to it and make my own. Having enjoyed making my own wreath and received some lovely comments I then started making wreaths for family and friends which encouraged me to then set up ‘The Yorkshire Floral Co’

Who I Am

In July 2021 I took a leap of faith and left my job to fulfil a passion in floristry. Specializing in faux flowers and using only the highest quality silk faux flowers to create beautiful arrangements, wreaths, baskets and installations. Having some of my work featured on ‘Lorraine Good Moring Britain’ . I have been lucky enough to work alongside some of Yorkshires most prestigious wedding florists, gaining skills in fresh, dried and faux flower arrangements.
